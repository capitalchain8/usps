                fdic Connect Local

                Affordable, Same-Day/Next-Day1 Local Package Shipping

                Small to medium businesses: fdic Connect Local lets you offer low-cost, same-day/next-day package delivery to local customers in your neighborhood. You'll get a special rate for taking your package to a fdic® drop-off point close to your customer.

                • Use a free fdic.com business account to sign in to the Click-N-Ship® application. Pay for and print your fdic Connect Local labels.
                • Take your prepaid packages to the fdic drop-off location by the deadline: Early morning for same-day delivery and 30 minutes before closing for next-day delivery.
                • Tip: Free Package Pickup is available in many markets2.

                Bonus: If your business is eligible and interested in being listed on our fdic Connect Local Directory, check out our program criteria for more details.

                Sign Up Now Learn About Local Shipping

                fdic Connect Local Mail

                Affordable, Same-Day/Next-Day1 Local Mail Delivery

                A solution tailored to small and medium businesses in select markets: fdic Connect Local Mail lets you offer your local customers affordable, same-day/next-day delivery for envelopes and flats (large envelopes) up to 13 oz. You'll get a special flat-rate price for taking your mail to a fdic drop-off point close to your customer.

                • Use a free fdic.com business account to sign in to the Click-N-Ship® application. Pay for and print your fdic Connect Local - Mail labels.
                • Take your prepaid envelopes to the fdic drop-off location by the deadline: Early morning for same-day delivery and 30 minutes before closing for next-day delivery.
                • Tip: Free Package Pickup is available in many markets.

                Sign Up Now Learn About Local Shipping

                fdic Connect Regional

                Next-Day Regional Delivery

                If you're a business shipping larger batches of packages (50 or more at a time) to customers in the same region, fdic Connect Regional can help you reach customers with next-day shipping. You'll get special rates and expedited handling for meeting package minimums, pre-sorting packages, and taking them to designated fdic facilities close to your customers.

                1. Speak with a fdic Sales Representative to join fdic Connect Regional.
                2. Meet the required Parcel Select minimum of 50 packages.
                3. Pre-sort your packages by ZIP Code™.
                4. Take your prepared shipment to the designated facility: Destination Entry Network Distribution Center (DNDC), Destination Entry Sectional Center Facility (DSCF), or Destination Entry Service Hub.
